Netaji Subhas University

Netaji Subhas University (NSU) in Jamshedpur is a private university established under the Jharkhand State Private University Act, 2018. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by various regulatory bodies such as AICTE, NCTE, BCI, PCI, and INC, ensuring that it maintains high academic standards across all disciplines. NSU offers a wide range of academic programs including diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral (PhD) courses. Fields of study include Engineering, Management, Law, Information Technology, Science, Education, Nursing, Pharmacy, Commerce, Arts, and Mass Communication.

The university strongly emphasizes research, practical and experiential learning, and the holistic development of students. It provides a vibrant campus environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern laboratories, and well-equipped libraries. NSU is dedicated to nurturing responsible, skilled professionals through quality education, innovative teaching methodologies, and comprehensive student support systems.
